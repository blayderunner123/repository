Here you will find all my projects I am working on. Keep in mind that they are test projects and may not be complete builds (Thanks to Windows *.1 Stripping files on transfers)

want a project simply clone it to your desktop.

  1. navigate cmd.exe on your PC and right-click then select run as administrator
  2. Then navigate to the directory you want by typing in $ cd C:/file/path/you/want/as/parent/
    • then type in: $ git clone "https://github.com/blayderunner123/repository.git"
    • then press [ENTER]
    • Then watch it flow (could take a few minutes to complete) Be patient!!
